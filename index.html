who are we?

Tickets Bling S.A. is the leading global ticketing company, operating in over 20 global markets, providing ticket sales, marketing, and distribution for tennis events. Tickets Bling S.A. celebrated its 30th anniversary in 2023 and currently serves more than 10,000 customers worldwide across multiple event categories, providing exclusive ticketing services to the finest arenas and stadiums hosting the most important tournaments on the ATP Tour.
